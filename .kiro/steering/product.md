# Product Overview

VIVK (Virtual Intelligent Versatile Knowledge) is India's smartest AI assistant platform designed specifically for the Indian market. The product provides an affordable alternative to international AI tools like ChatGPT Plus, offering the same advanced AI capabilities at 70% lower cost.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Affordable Pricing**: Starting at ₹499/month vs ₹1,650+ for international alternatives
- **Indian Context**: Built to understand Indian holidays, festivals, business culture, and local languages
- **Local Support**: Support during Indian time zones with understanding of local needs
- **Advanced AI**: Powered by Claude Sonnet for paid users, Claude Haiku for free users

## Target Market

- Entrepreneurs and small business owners
- Content creators and marketers
- Developers and students
- HR professionals and consultants
- E-commerce businesses

## Subscription Tiers

1. **Free**: ₹0 - 20 queries/day, basic AI model, 7-day chat history
2. **Pro**: ₹499/month - Unlimited queries, advanced AI model, unlimited history, API access
3. **Business**: ₹2,999/month - Everything in Pro + team features, custom integrations, dedicated support

## Key Features

- AI-powered conversations with context preservation
- Content creation (emails, blogs, social media)
- Business automation (contracts, presentations, reports)
- Coding assistance and debugging
- Learning and research support
- Multi-language support (Hindi, regional languages coming soon)
